This is me 

Hi, my name is Roxana Silaghi, I'm a student at Faculty of Political Administrative and Communication sciences on Digital media and Game studies master program. In case you didn't read what I sad above, I love taking pictures and edit them. I find it very relaxing. When a take pictures a like them to be as natural as they can be. I don't want people to feel stressed about posing around me and I want to show them that no matter who you are, you can be photogenic if you feel yorself enough. I don't take just pictures of people, a also enjoy photographing nature and architecture which catches my eye. If you look very closely around you will discover that the world is not that boring and grey.
